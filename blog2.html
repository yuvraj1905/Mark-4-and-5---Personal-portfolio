Online shopping: The new Normal

Online shopping has become very common among people all around the world. From smartphones, to clothes, to daily necessities, groceries, and what not, the list goes huge. The new add on technologies empowering online shopping like AI visualisation and 3d modelling using machine learning and other technologies have brought the gap to a larger extent. For example, even the Eye glasses can be purchased online with 3d visualisation and eye care functionalities taken in consideration. This phase has led to an era where revolutionary change to people's perception about shopping has been taking place over the time.

Why Online over Offline?

Online shopping offers variety of features and offers to attract and ease customers to opt for it over the traditional offline shopping. Almost everyone has the access to internet and that is the only prerequisite to use online shopping apps and websites. Online shopping provides the ease of choosing the required article among tons of options for the customer onto their fingertips. The purchased item gets delivered to the user's location within a certain time. Users have the categorised and easy to use interface to assist them with their preferences to shop. There are features like price sorting, filters categorisation, image search, and many more which provide the user the options to choose avoiding to their budget and preferences accurately. Moreover, one of the main attractions for online buyers is the discount and offers that is offered by these apps and websites. There are huge discounts offered specially at times of festivals. There are return policies and customer support too which makes online shopping perfect and flawless for everyone.
