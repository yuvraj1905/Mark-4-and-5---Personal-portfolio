PUBG mobile: Its rise, popularity and Ban in india

Players Unknown Battleground Mobile aka PUBG is regarded as the base of eSports rise in India. PUBG mobile is the mobile version of previously launched computer version of the game PUBG PC.

The rise:The Game was launched back in 2018 , the phase where smartphones were getting more feasible and popular among people in India. PUBG mobile is a multiplayer battle royale game with attractive gaming interface, features like communicating in-game with friends over voice, etc . The competitive nature of the game brought attraction to many of the Gaming streamers all over the world to play the game and stream it over social platforms like YouTube, Facebook, twitch , etc. This eventually brought the game to popularize at an extensive rate.

The eSports Career drive: PUBG soon got started to be played on a global level as a competitive tournament. The tournaments were held on national and international levels too, were teams of 4 people could join, compete and win immense cash prizes. This brought many gaming organizations to tie up with players to play under their name and offer salaries for the same. This meta started attracting more and more people all over the country. People started opting for a full time career in this field as a role of eSports athelete.

The BAN: In September 2020, the indian government banned 100+ applications from the Android and ios platforms, in which PUBG was included too. The reason was specified as the data of users were accessible to the chinese authorities. This action took the whole eSports community to shock. Many eSports organizations, communities started to dismantle. But soon, the decision was accepted and respected by people as it was claimed as a national threat. People started opting for better opportunities for themselves in different fields accordingly.
